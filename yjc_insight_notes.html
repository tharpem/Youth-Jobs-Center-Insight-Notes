Overall Curriculum

There were some notes by our curriculum reviewer that she wanted to share.

  • Substantive content which flows nicely
  • Curriculum clearly designed to be engaging for students
  • If executed correctly, should assist students in creating a clear path to college/career and ultimately economic security.
  • She ♥ the Intern Orientation section.
  • Some good opportunities were presented to internalize the goal planning process

    • General Curriculum and Program Questions

    Is there a continued communication with students that have successfully completed the program?

    How long and how?

    What is the typical demographic of students that access the curriculum?

    Do diverse learners have access to the curriculum?

    Are they a targeted population as well? How have they done with the curriculum?

    Approximately how long is each lesson?

    Is the majority of that time spent on the lesson or doing other things like behavior management or housekeeping? Does the session length seem like the right length?

    Year One (9th Grade)

    Have students ever given push back to completing the worksheets?

    If so, what is the alternative?

    Year Two (10th Grade)

    Is 10th grade content a continuation of 9th grade curriculum?

    The lessons begin with lesson 4 and there only 3 lessons. Is this actually considered year two? - which would make sense.

    Would any student new to the program begin with the 9th grade curriculum or would they start at their grade level? If the latter is true, then some tweaking would need to be done to ensure a sophomore doesn’t miss some of the crucial lessons from year one.

    The session following the Introduction is Workplace Culture and Etiquette.However at the conclusion of the Introduction it is stated “At our next session, we will discuss our personal values and how they impact of professional decisions and growth.” Is this correct?

    Year Three (11th Grade)

    The 1st session refers to previous week’s research but there is no lesson plan for that week in file. How did students conduct research?

    Is this a continuation from year 2? Can 11 grade curriculum stand alone or would a year 3 student begin with the 9th grade curriculum?

    Year Four (12th Grade)

    Post Secondary Goals and Planning section is listed in both session 5 and session 6 with the same verbiage.  

    In Session 5, the Post Secondary Goals and Planning section is followed by Career Exploration. In Session 6, the Post Secondary Goals and Planning section is followed by the Career Profile section.  Is this intentional?
